The International Association Of Human Rights & Equality Activism

V FOR HUMAN is an international NGO that advocates for human rights and gender equality through education, intellectual growth, and community engagement.

We strive to create a positive sustainable social impact by empowering individuals to advocate for their rights and promoting inclusion and equality for all, while also fostering intellectual self-development through our educational programs and independent projects.
